Artificial intelligence (AI):

the ability of a digital computer or computer-controlled robot to perform tasks commonly associated with intelligent beings,

for example, discovering proofs for mathematical theorems or playing chessâ€”with great proficiency.

All but the simplest human behaviour is ascribed to intelligence, while even the most complicated insect behaviour is never taken as an indication of intelligence.

On the other hand, some programs have attained the performance levels of human experts and professionals in performing certain specific tasks,

so that artificial intelligence in this limited sense is found in applications as diverse as medical diagnosis, computer search engines, and voice or handwriting recognition.
